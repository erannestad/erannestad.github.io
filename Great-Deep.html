The Great Deep


2020 - current

___

+ show statement

In biblical cosmology a firmament physically separated earth from the heavens and oceans above. These paintings imagine a reality where the structures built in the wake of climate change and environmental collapse have become our world’s new firmaments. The earth’s atmosphere is as much a human-built structure as the biodomes and greenhouses we need to grow our food.

The gridding and organic components of the work suggest that our natural and built worlds are intertwined: precarious systems on the verge of collapse. The paintings in the Firmament Series render these systems into collisions of weather, climate infrastructure, and perspective -- a call to the sublime.
