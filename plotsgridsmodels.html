Plots, Grids, Models
2022-Current

+ show statement

Layered in painting space are various literal and abstract variables used in the econometric models that justify and plan interventions in our landscape, our atmosphere, our economies. Selections, masks, coordinate systems, and images associated with each investigation are rendered by hand, or by drawing machine, and placed within the painting’s grid system.
