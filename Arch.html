Firmament Arch describes the designed and constructed qualities of climate change and the structure of our relationship to the natural world post ecological collapse. These works reimagine cosmological structures of the past: the pillars of heaven, the firmament, the great deep, the ocean of heaven, etc. as architectural backbones of our future infrastructure and defining forces in our relationship to nature.

Completed with Clowes Fund Fellowship at the Vermont Studio Center. April 2019.
