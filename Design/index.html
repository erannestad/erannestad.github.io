Eric Rannestad is a design strategist, researcher and artist. His work uses design as a tool to translate complex ideas into engaging and usable information.

Eric is an alumnus of the New York Arts Practicum. He has attended residencies at Shell House Arts, Vermont Studio Center, and North Cascades Institute. Eric holds a BA in Economics and Studio Art from Whitman College in Washington (US) and has studied at the Glasgow School of Art (UK).

Eric lives in the Pacific Northwest where he has worked for ECONorthwest, The Nature Conservancy, and Washington State Conservation Districts.

Feel free to get in touch and say hello.
